    Terms and Condtions

    CHS Absence Tool is an online service that allows credit hours engineering students in Cairo University to check and track their absence in the college throughout the semester, to help them avoid deprivation from any course.

    1. This is a non-official site and has no relation to “Cairo University Main Administration” or “Cairo University Faculty of Engineering Administration”, it is completely created by students in the mentioned college, depending on informal information from administration.”

    2. The service only works for “Cairo University Faculty of Engineering, Credit Hours System”, and it is not designed to check absence of any other college, institute, or even mainstream section in “Cairo University Faculty of Engineering”.

    3. This is a non-profit website, and it is totally free; you will not be asked to pay anything while using the site.

    4. This is an open-source project, anyone is licensed to clone the same idea, design, and code files for any purpose, provided referring to the original creators of this project, click here to access the files.
